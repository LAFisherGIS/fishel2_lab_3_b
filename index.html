Pierce County Real Estate Considerations

Pierce County Census Tract Level Vacancy Rates

This map provides for the easy estimation of how easy it would be to find a property available for rental or purchase within the various census tracts of Pierce County Washington, as well as the degree of choice one might experience in these areas. This estimation is facilitated through reference to data which report on the vacancy rates within each separate census tract.

  • 0% - 2.964255% Vacancy
  • 2.964255% - 3.877140% Vacancy
  • 3.877140% - 4.930006% Vacancy
  • 4.930006% - 6.693878% Vacancy
  • 6.93878%+ Vacancy

About the Analysis

Census tract polygons were converted from a shapefile to a geojson with only 7.6% of the geometric complexity remaining using Mapshaper and wrapped up as a javascript variable prior to inclusion in the map. Based on their registered vacancy rates, these polygons have then been quantified into five contiguous groups containing equal numbers of polygons (quintiles), and colored according to their vacancy rates on a red to blue scale, with the deepest blue representing the highest amount of vacant units on the market. Data regarding the total numbers of housing units are available by scrolling over a specific census tract, and will appear in the top right corner. If you click on a census tract, you will automatically zoom in and center the map on its center, allowing for more targeted evaluation of its suitability for your needs based on the points of interested located within it.
